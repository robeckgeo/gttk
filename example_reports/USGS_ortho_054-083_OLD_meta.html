                📝 procdesc Multiple aircraft were utilized in the St. Louis region in the Spring of 2015 for digital image acquistion during leaf-off conditions. Cessna Conquests equipped with Leica ADS100 Airborne Digital Sensors captured 4 band imagery over the project area. Imagery was captured at an altitude of 4,800' above mean terrain for the project area. All four bands (rgb natural color plus near infrared) of raw camera data was processed using Leica X-Pro software for Aerotriangulation and Rectification coupled with Surdex proprietary image handling software for color correction and dodging. Post processing of Airborne and Ground GPS data was performed with Inertial Explorer and rectification of imagery (to a variety of sources from 10m DEM to 3d stereo collection) was completed using Surdex proprietary software . Image mosaicking, QC and final DOQQ products were produced using proprietary Surdex software. All deliverables meet or exceed standards for horizontal accuracy for well defined points.
